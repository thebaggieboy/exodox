What is Exodox?

Exodox is a technology company specialising in the development of AI technology, blockchain technology and e-commerce space with its base in Manchester, UK. Artificial intelligence has changed and is changing our way of life. It has replaced numerous manual and labour-intensive jobs. Blockchain Technology has made it easy for us to move money in seconds. It has eradicated the high fees banks charge to move money. E-commerce has made buying and selling very easy you won’t have to leave your comfort zone to buy or sell a product. .

Collating these 3 elements; AI, Blockchain Technology and E-commerce, Exodox was birthed. Exodox is here to bridge the gap between manufacturers and consumers. With Exodox, you can get products at a cheaper price with the best quality and also make passive income on every product you buy. With the launch of our official website, users will now be able to earn passively- You buy a product on our website and we resell it for you at a better price. Note that you don’t have to look for consumers to buy your products. Exodox will use AI technology to match your product with potential buyers. .

Exodox is fully funded by our partners- meaning we don’t take profits from you. Although, 10% will be deducted from profits made on every trade to pay our employees and ensure the smooth running of the website. Our mission is clear- to create an opportunity for passive income, bridge the gap between manufacturers and consumers and also massively influence the cryptocurrency space. Let’s join Exodox to lead the progress of humanity by using Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain Technology and E-commerce to bring benefits to our community. Sign up on Exodox today, to join the moving train
